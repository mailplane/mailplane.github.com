How to uninstall the unsupported Google Gears input manager.

Google Gears isn't supported anymore. Here's how you can unisntall Google Gears:

  • Quit Mailplane
  • In Finder, open your Applications folder.
  • Open the folder named Utilities.
  • Open Terminal.
  • In Terminal, enter the following command (including the quotes): '/Library/Internet Plug-Ins/Gears.plugin/Contents/Resources/uninstall.command'
  • Press Enter.
  • Enter your password when prompted.
