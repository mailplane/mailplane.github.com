Mailplane 2 can be run on any OS X starting at Tiger (10.4) up to Mountain Lion (10.8). Mailplane 3 requires Lion (10.7) or Mountain Lion (10.8).

If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us at support@mailplaneapp.com.com or via the Help > Support and Feedback menu item.
