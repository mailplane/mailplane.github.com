--- layout: help_post title: 'Mailplane is crashing, why?' author: Ruben category: help permalink: /faq/entry/mailplane_is_crashing_why/index.html tags: - faq ---

Some users experience crashes because of one of these two InputManagers: "Safari Ad Blocker" and "SafariBlock". Solution: Uninstall these "Safari Ad Blocker" and "SafariBlock" and restart Mailplane. Or use the new "Use System WebKit" advanced preference.

If Mailplane is still crashing, please report your crash to us.
