macOS Mojave Dark Mode: Join the DARK SIDE! Mailplane automatically adapts Gmail, Calendar, Contacts in dark mode.

Dismiss Authorization Banner: Use Mailplane including new mail notifications without granting permission on sign-in. Especially useful for those who are not able to grant permission due to company limitations.

Save Clip as PDF File: Save message as PDF directly via 'Save Clip'. This is a great alternative to 'File > Print > PDF > Save PDF'.

Hiver Extension: Use Gmail for customer support with the newly supported Hiver extension.

Paste Files: 'Edit > Paste' can now paste files too. You don't need to use the separate 'Edit > Paste File' menu item anymore.

Upgrade your License: Upgrade your personal to a family license or add further seats to your site license directly via 'Mailplane > License...'.

OmniFocus 3: 'File > Save Clip...' now supports OmniFocus 3.
