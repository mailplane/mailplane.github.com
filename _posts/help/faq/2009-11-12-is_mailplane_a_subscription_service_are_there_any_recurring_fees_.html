--- layout: help_post title: 'Is Mailplane a subscription service? Are there any recurring fees? ' author: Ruben category: help permalink: /faq/entry/is_mailplane_a_subscription_service_are_there_any_recurring_fees_/index.html tags: - faq ---

No, Mailplane is not a subscription service. You pay for a license to use Mailplane 2.x - it's not time limited in any way and there are no recurring fees.

You don't have to pay for any 2.x updates, they are all included in the purchase price. That said, Mailplane 3.0 will be a payed upgrade. Registered users will the opportunity to buy a discounted license.
