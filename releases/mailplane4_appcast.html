Performance Improvements: We improved the typing when composing an email and keyboard shortcut performance.

Open downloads automatically: Mailplane doesn't open Finder anymore after an attachment has been downloaded. You can enable 'Open downloads automatically' in 'Preferences > General' to let it open the default app automatically.

Rename attachments prior to downloading: Attachments can now be renamed prior to downloading and file dialog keyboard shortcuts will now work properly.

Open Google Drive in web browser: There's a new setting in 'Preferences > Accounts > Advanced' which allows you to open Google Drive in your default web browser.

Save to Google Drive: Save to Google Drive can now be accessed by holding the Option key while hovering over the attachment.

Context Menu: Added 'Copy Link', 'Copy Email Address', 'Open Link', and 'Download Link' to the context menu.
