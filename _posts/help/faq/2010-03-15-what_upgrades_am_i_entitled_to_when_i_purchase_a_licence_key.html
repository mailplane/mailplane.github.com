--- layout: help_post title: 'What upgrades am I entitled to when I purchase a licence key?' author: Ruben category: help permalink: /faq/entry/what_upgrades_am_i_entitled_to_when_i_purchase_a_licence_key/index.html tags: - faq ---

A version x license key is valid for all version x releases of the software. For example a version 2.x licence key will work for versions 2.0.0 and 2.1.6 of the software. When we publish a new major release after your purchase you will be able to upgrade at discounted price.

If you want to be informed of future versions you should subscribe to our newsletter.
