Mailplane 4 is here!

It is packed with new features and looks better than ever.

  • Annotation & Shapes: Get your point across with fewer words using annotations and shapes.
  • Notifications with Actions: Archive a message or write a quick reply directly from new email notifications.
  • New Extensions: Extend Gmail and become more productive.
  • Search Everywhere: Search all your accounts at once.
  • ... and more

Unfortunately you're not able to install it on your Mac. Make sure to run at least macOS 10.12+ (Sierra).

This will be a paid upgrade but you can trial it 15 days for free. If you don't like it, you can revert back to Mailplane 3 at any time.
