This update allows Mailplane 2 to start on Yosemite (OS X 10.10) but we cannot offer any insight as to how long this update will last as it depends on Gmail and OS X changes.

So you may eventually notice that some features stop working. We hope you understand that we can't support Mailplane 2 indefinitely. See also our article here and don't hesitate to let us know if you have any questions.
