If you are having problems logging in to one account and/or sending/archiving emails from one account, please try clearing out the cookies stored within Mailplane as follows:

1) Close Mailplane 2) In Finder navigate to /Users//Library/Application Support/Mailplane (Option click "GO" menu then click "Library") If you are using Lion, you need to hold the 'option' key while clicking 'GO" to reveal the Library 3) Move all files ending with the word .cookies to the Trash (do not remove any other file!) 4) Start Mailplane

If this does not solve the issue, please contact us for customer support: support@mailplaneapp.com
