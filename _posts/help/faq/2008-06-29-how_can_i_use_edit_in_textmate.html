--- layout: help_post title: 'How can I use “Edit in TextMate”?' author: Ruben category: help permalink: /faq/entry/how_can_i_use_edit_in_textmate/index.html tags: - faq ---

  • Install the TextMate InputManager by following the steps outlined in TextMate Help.
  • In Mailplane click on the "Plain Text" above the body text field. Rich Text editing isn't supported by the TextMate InputManager. Gmail will remember the setting, BTW.
  • Now use the "Edit->Edit in Textmate" menu item or press (Cmd-Control-E) to open the current message in TextMate
