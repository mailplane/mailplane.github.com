Setup Mailplane to start when you login your Mac.

Follow these steps, if you like to open Mailplane when you start your Mac or sign into your Mac account:

  1. Start Mailplane and make sure it shows in your Dock
  2. Control-Click the Mailplane icon
  3. Choose Options
  4. Make sure Open at Login is checked

Tip: Hide main window

If you like to hide Mailplane's main window at login, try this:

  1. Open System Preferences > Accounts > Login Items
  2. Add Mailplane if it's not in the list
  3. Check "Hide" next to the Mailplane entry
