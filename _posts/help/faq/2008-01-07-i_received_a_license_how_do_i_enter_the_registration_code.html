--- layout: help_post title: 'I received a license, how do I enter the registration code?' author: Ruben category: help permalink: /faq/entry/i_received_a_license_how_do_i_enter_the_registration_code/index.html tags: - faq ---

  1. Open Mailplane
  2. Locate the license email in your inbox. The subject is Mailplane license for
  3. Scroll down to the attachment section
  4. Click the Download link next to the Your License.mailplanelicense
  5. Done!
