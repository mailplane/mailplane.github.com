Learn how to buy and license Mailplane.

How to buy Mailplane

Please visit the Mailplane store to buy your Mailplane license. There are three different licenses to choose from:

  • Single user license: Our most popular license: A single-user license for Mailplane 2.x that you can install on any Mac you personally use.
  • Family license: Best license for families: Allow up to five (5) family members living in the same household to use Mailplane 2.x on their Macs.
  • Site license: The right choice for companies or institutions: Get a Mailplane 2.x site license for multiple users. Quantity discount offered.

A few minutes after payment, you'll get one license email.

How to install the License

Here is how you install the license:

  1. If needed: Forward the license email to your Gmail account
  2. In Mailplane: Open the License email it has the subject "Mailplane license for â€¦"
  3. Scroll down to the attachment
  4. Click the "download" link

To install the license on your other Mac or under a different Mac account: Repeat procedure above.

How to install a family or site license:

If you bought the family license or site license, please forward the license email to your family members or co-workers and repeat the installation described above.
