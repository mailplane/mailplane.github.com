Create New Gmail Account

Gmail has much to offer: 8 GB of free space, labels instead of folders, conversations, integration with many services and much more. There are two editions to choose from:

gmail.com domain

Register for a regular and free Gmail account. Your Gmail address will be like yourname@gmail.com.

your own domain

If you prefer to have your own domain, then apply for a Google Apps account here. The Gmail address will then be yourname@yourdomain.com, where yourdomain.com will be a name you pick.

Mailplane for Mac

Mailplane is the best way to use your new Gmail account on a Mac.

Download Mailplane Now

Use your new account with Mailplane for Mac

After successful registration, return to Mailplane and enter your new email address and password into either the Authentication dialog or Accounts preference page.
