--- layout: help_post title: 'How do I report a crash?' author: Ruben category: help permalink: /faq/entry/how_do_i_report_a_crash/index.html tags: - faq ---

Crashes shouldn't happen, and we'll do everything to solve them. They can be caused by a Mailplane bug, a misconfiguration of the Mac, or other software like InputManagers. The crash report is extremly important for fixing the cause of the crash.

Mailplane make it easy to send use the full crash report: When you restart Mailplane after a crash, you'll be asked to submit the crash report. Tip: If you remember which steps led to the crash you can enter this in the comment section.
