Accounts

Up to 10 simultaneous accounts are supported. Switch between them instantly.

Calendar

Google Calendar is a perfect match for Gmail. Mailplane combines them in one app. No calendar set-up required.

Integration

OSX-like keyboard shortcuts, a Toolbar, Mail PDF from every Print Dialog, OSX Services, mailto:, AppleScript.

Enhance Gmail

Enable great plug-ins (Rapportive, Boomerang, AwayFind, ToutApp), hide ads or apply a custom stylesheet easily.

Attachments

Scale images, compress directories, capture screenshots, make photos and attach it to your message. No need to start another app.

Notifier

Mailplane's notifier works out of the box and tells you everything about unread messages. And of course it integrates with OSX's notification center. But the best part is the 'Do Not Disturb' mode which allows you to stay focused.

Social animal

Mailplane talks to other MacOS apps like Evernote, OmniFocus, iWork suite, Safari, iPhoto, Contacts. See a full list here.
