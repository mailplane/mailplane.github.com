--- layout: help_post title: 'Emails are slow to arrive to my inbox' author: Jessica category: help tags: - pending_issue ---

Sometimes some users are finding that they will get a notification of a new message and it takes awhile to show up in the inbox. You can access the message quickly by clicking the notification directly, or clicking View > Reload Page (this should help speed up a slow message).

We are aware of this issue and working hard to get our syncing time better. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us directly at: support@mailplaneapp.com.com
