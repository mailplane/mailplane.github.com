Regular Gmail

Create a regular and free Gmail account. Your Gmail address will be yourname@gmail.com.

Google Apps for Business

Apply for a Google Apps for Business account if you prefer to have your own domain. Your address will then be yourname@yourdomain.com.

Mailplane for Mac

Mailplane is the best way to use Gmail on your Mac. Learn more here.
