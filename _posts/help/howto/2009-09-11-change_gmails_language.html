Gmail comes in dozens of languages; learn how to switch your Gmail interface to another locale.

Gmail's language setting works independently from Mailplane's.

Here's how you can change it:

  1. Important: Save & close all unsaved drafts.
  2. In the Main Window click the Settings link
  3. Choose the desired language in the General tab.
  4. Save changes (button is at bottom of screen).
