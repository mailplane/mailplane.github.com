New Google Calendar
Google started rolling-out a new Google Calendar last month. Mailplane does now officially support it. Read more about it on their blog.

Gmail Add-ons
Google added the possibility to write Add-ons for Gmail. Simply click the settings wheel on the top right of your inbox and then "Get add-ons". Read more about it on their blog.

Fixes and Improvements
  • Fixed Rapportive which was off the right side of the screen.
  • Fixed font rendering on macOS 10.13 (High Sierra).
  • Ignore 'Hide Right-Hand Column' preference if Streak is enabled.
  • Fixed the case where it did open links with cyrillic characters in Mailplane.
  • Updated to Streak 6.30.
  • Fixed a number of crashes.
