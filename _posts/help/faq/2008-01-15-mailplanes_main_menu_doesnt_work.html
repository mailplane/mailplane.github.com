--- layout: help_post title: 'Mailplane’s main menu doesn’t work' author: Ruben category: help permalink: /faq/entry/mailplanes_main_menu_doesnt_work/index.html tags: - faq ---

Some users reported that Cocoa Gestures does interfere with Mailplane in a way that makes the main menu unusable. Disable or remove Cocoa Gestures to solve the problem.
