--- layout: help_post title: 'Is there a Mailplane version for the iPhone and/or iPad?' author: Ruben category: help permalink: /faq/entry/is_there_a_mailplane_version_for_the_iphone_andor_ipad/index.html tags: - faq ---

Mailplane is a Mac-only application. There are currently no plans for an iPhone or iPad version.

Tip: Try Mailroom for the iPhone. It is an excellent Gmail client for the iPhone providing multiple accounts and access to your iPhone address book.
