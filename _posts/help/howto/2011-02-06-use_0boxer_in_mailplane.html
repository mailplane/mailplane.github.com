Please note that 0Boxer is no longer supported in Mailplane 2.5.x. Sorry for the inconvenience.

0boxer turns "inbox zero" into a game. You get points when you answer, archive, or delete email.

0Boxer is a plugin for Gmail. It turns "inbox zero" into a game. You get points when you answer, archive, or delete email.

To enable 0boxer:

  1. Open Preferences
  2. Click "Accounts" then "Plug-Ins"
  3. Enable "0boxer"
  4. You'll see the 0boxer bar above your inbox.
  5. Click the "Sign-In" link and Grant access.

To disable 0boxer:

  1. Open Preferences
  2. Click "Accounts" then "Plug-Ins"
  3. Enable "0boxer"
  4. Select View > Reload Page from the menu.
