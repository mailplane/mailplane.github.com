Accounts

Switch between accounts instantly. No need to sign-out and sign-in.

For up to three accounts we suggest to use tabs and beyond that you can use the Switch to account... feature.

Calendar

Google Calendar is a perfect match for Gmail. Mailplane combines them in one app.

No separate calendar set-up required and accepting an invitation intuitively switches to the correct calendar tab.

Integration

Mailplane is a native Mac OSX application and integrates naturally into Mac OSX.

Apple Mail like keyboard shortcuts, acting as default Mail Client, Mail PDF from Print Dialog, OSX Services and much more.

Enhance Gmail

Enable great Gmail plugins on an account basis with a single click.

And hide ads or apply a custom stylesheet.

Attachments

Resize images, compress directories or capture your screen and attach it to your message. No need to start another app.

Don't download attachments twice. Preview them with QuickLook.

Notifier

Mailplane's notifier tells you how many unread messages are in your inboxes, right from your Mac menu bar.

It does also integrate with Apple's notification center and the 'Do Not Disturb' mode allows you to stay focused.

Social animal

Mailplane talks to apps like Safari, iWork suite, iPhoto, Contacts, Evernote, Things, OmniFocus, Alfred and Lyn.

Mailplane has also an AppleScript interface to automate some of your daily workflows. More here.
