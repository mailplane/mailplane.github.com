How to copy all account settings like signatures, text snippets to another Mac.

Mailplane stores are its accounts settings in a single file. The file contains text snippets, signatures and the account options. If you use Mailplane on multiple Macs, you can avoid reentering the account information:

  1. Open the HOME/Library/Application Support/Mailplane folder
  2. Copy the Accounts_2.xml file to the other Mac (Do not copy the other files, as they contain "session" data)

Other settings:

  • Passwords: Are stored in the login keychain.
  • General Preferences: Are stored in HOME/Library/Preferences/com.mailplaneapp.Mailplane.plist
