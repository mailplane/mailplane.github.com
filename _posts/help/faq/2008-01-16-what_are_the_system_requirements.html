Mailplane needs MAC OS X 10.4 (Tiger), 10.5 (Leopard), 10.6 (Snow Leopard), or 10.7 (Lion) to run. As it is a Universal Binary it does run on both PowerPC and Intel Macs. Please make sure to download the version that matches your Mac. Just visit the download page to see all available downloads.
