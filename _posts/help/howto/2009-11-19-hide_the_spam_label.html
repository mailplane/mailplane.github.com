Gmail now offers the option to organize the sidebar and hide the Spam label.

To hide the Spam counter in your sidebar:

  • Click the Settings link in the upper right of the main window
  • Select the Labels tab
  • Click Hide next to Spam

To see or select the Spam folder, click xx more link in the sidebar:

Tip: Navigate to any label with your keyboard

Press Command-Option-G to open Mailplane's label and quick link navigator.
