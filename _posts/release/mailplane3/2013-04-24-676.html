Password Is Now Optional
You don't need to store your account password anymore. Just leave it empty inside "Preferences... > Accounts > General".

Sign Out
Due to technical reasons we can't provide an individual account sign-out. But you can now sign-out all tabs via "Mailplane 3 > Sign Out".

Open Link Externally
Press Command-Shift while clicking a link to force Mailplane open it in your default browser.

Fixes
* Notifier required frequent re-authorization.
* Paste image attaches it to prevent loosing it while sending.
* Crashes after starting Mailplane the first time.
