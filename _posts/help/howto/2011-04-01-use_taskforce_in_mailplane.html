Use Taskforce to convert emails into tasks, so you can save time and stay organized.

This plugin and service is provided by Taskforceapp.com.

  1. To enable the plugin, go to Preferences > Account > Plug-Ins and turn on "Taskforce".
  2. Click the Taskforce "Sign-up" or "Login" button to get started.
