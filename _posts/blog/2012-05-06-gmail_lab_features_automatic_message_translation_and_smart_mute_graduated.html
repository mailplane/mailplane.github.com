Google has graduated (they are now part of Gmail) three lab features.

  • Automatic Message Translation
  • Title Tweaks
  • Smart Mute

I like the "Automatic Message Translation" most :)

Other lab feature were less successful and have been retired:

  • Old Snakey
  • Mail Goggles
  • Mouse Gestures
  • Hide Unread Counts
  • Move Icon Column
  • Inbox Preview
  • Custom Date Format
  • SMS in Chat gadget (SMS is still working, but the lab feature has been removed).
